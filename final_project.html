How to Play:

  • Click the "Roll Dice" button to start the game.
  • Click the "Roll Dice" button again to advance to the next round.
  • The game ends after three rounds and whoever has the higher final score wins!
  • Click "New Game" if you want to play again!

Rules:

  • You and the computer will play a game consisting of 3 rounds
  • Each round you and the computer will roll a pair or dice and the score for that round will be the sum of the dice.
  • Your score for the round will be added to your total score.
  • If you roll doubles, your score is doubled (example 2 and 2 will get you a score of 8).
  • If you roll a 1, your score for the round is automatically 0.
  • The player with the highest total score at the end of the 3 rounds wins the game!
